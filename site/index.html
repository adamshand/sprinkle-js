But why?

  • No bundler

    Modern web development often require you to go through thousands of configurations to setup your bundler and this is often the only way to have a bit of reactivity in your app. The aim of Sprinkle JS is to free you of all of this!

  • Small size

    With a bundle size of less than 3kb gzipped and no additional dependencies Sprinkle JS will save your time without wasting your users time!

  • Powerful primitives

    A few powerful primitives it's all what is exported from Sprinkle JS. You might not have the flexibility of React, Vue, Svelte or Solid but let's be honest: for your fun little app you don't need all of that! Let Sprinkle take care of DOM updates and write your app ina breeze.
